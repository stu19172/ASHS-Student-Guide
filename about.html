What is tutorial class? It is a class that occurs every Tuesday and Thursday, during period 1. Tutorial class is meant to give students the opportunity to catch with their learning. However, tutorial teachers are also meant to help them pass Level 1 to Level 3 NCEA, in order to help everyone's future.
